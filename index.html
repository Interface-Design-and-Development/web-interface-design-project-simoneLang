About Us

Hi! I'm Simone, founder of Crunchy Cravings. Our story began in my small college apartment in 2022, where late-night snacking experiments turned into wild flavor creations. After countless spicy, salty, and cheesy tests (and a few kitchen disasters), I knew chips could be more than a side snack; they could be the highlight of every gathering and the hero of every late-night craving.

Today, Crunchy Cravings crafts each batch with bold personality and big heart. We believe snacking should be an adventure, and every chip should bring a smile (and maybe a little happy dance). Thank you for crunching along with us; we can’t wait for you to find your new favorite flavor!
